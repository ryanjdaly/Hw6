About this app

How it works:

  • 5 key system: navigate using wasd, f to select
  • 3 key system: move to next item using 1, select with 2, 3 automatically selects "="
  • Testers completed 5 equations while being timed

Test results:

Tester 5 key time 3 key time 5 key errors 3 key errors
Brenna 1 min 9 sec 1 min 59 sec 3 5
Ryan 38 sec 1 min 1 3
Cassie 1 min 32 sec 1 min 29 sec 10 4

Interestingly the two testers who are familiar with wasd video games performed much better with the 5 key system than the 3, while the tester who was not performed slightly better with the 3 key system. While the 5 key system is more efficient it seems the 3 key system is simpler for people who don't type or play video games much.
